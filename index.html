GUIDED GROWTH

Text-based service offering daily goal-setting, progress tracking, and tools for sustainable personal growth

The Problem
We all are ambitious and want to live fuller lives, but often aren't able to stay motivated or do the things we seek on a daily basis. How can we easily and consistently incorporate personal development into our daily schedule?

Approach

  • Prioritize daily consistent action
  • Focus on ease of use and incorporate service within people's existing schedule
  • Provide meaningful progress updates and opportunities to pivot

Solution

  • Send texts in the morning for users' daily intention and implementation plan related to desired growth goal
  • Send text at the end of the day for progress update
  • Provide weekly progress summary, with periodic motivational content and surveys to pivot overarching growth goal

Results
After a 30 day pilot, over 70% of daily goals were accomplished and participants expressed greater control and motivation
